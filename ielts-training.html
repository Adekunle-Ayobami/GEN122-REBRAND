IELTS Training

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is designed to help you work, study or migrate to a country where English is the native language. This includes countries such as Australia, Canada, the UK, USA and other international countries.

FAQs

Why is IELTS important?
IELTS is important because it is one of the most widely accepted English language proficiency tests in the world. It is used by over 11,000 organizations in 140 countries for a variety of purposes, including: immigration, education, employment, ete.
Can IELTS be passed at one sitting?
Yes, IELTS can be passed at one sitting. Many people do pass the IELTS test on their first attempt. However, it is important to be well-prepared before taking the test. This means practicing all four sections of the test (Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking) regularly and getting feedback from an experienced IELTS teacher or tutor.
How long does it take for the IELTS result to be released?
IELTS results are typically available 13 days after the test date for paper-based tests and 3-5 days after the test date for computer-based tests. The results are released online and can be accessed through the Test Taker Portal. However, there are a few other factors that can affect how long it takes for IELTS results to be released.
