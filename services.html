Frequently Asked Questions

How long will this whole process take?
Between 3 to 6 months if there are no complications with the visa process. The job seeking process can also take time if presentation is not well done.
Will there be need for proof of fund?
Yes, if the employer does not offer this. Some employers can offer a relocation package, this will include flight, one month's accomodation but most of them will deduct from wages, in this case no proof of fund is required.
Does this process allow me to travel with another person?
As a married person, you can apply for your partner & children. However only one partner is recognized by the UK Government.
What are the conditions attached to the whole process?
You will need to meet all the requirements stipulated by the UK governemnt.
What are the Visa Requirements
  • Nigerian Passport
  • Visa Application fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge
  • IELTS Results or Proof of English Language Understanding
  • Letter of Sponsorship from Employer
  • Medical Tests Result (Tuberculosis/Hepatitis B) Proof of Funds
Is IELTS Compulsory?
Conflicting, but better to do this if you don't have GCSE in English/ University Degree. We hear of those who were exempted from IELTS , but our advice is to just do it, to tick the box, just in case your form is assessed by an inexperienced officer & they refuses the visa on that basis, the decision can be appealed but that will add at least another 6+ months to the visa process + additional costs.
what if my visa application is refused?
We advise that all requirements set by the Embassy is met, if any visa is refused, we will review the reasons for rejections and would advise if the appeal route is necessary.
Do I need a reference letter?
Yes, if your job schedule permits this - check in emplyment contract when offered.
Can someone take two jobs?
Officially no, your visa for the first few years is tied down to your sponsor.
