Healthcare Training Certificate

THE CARE CERTIFICATIONS UPDATES AND FULL COURSES (STANDARDS)

Care certifications are being obtained rapidly as the employment rate in the health care sector is increasing. There is a constant and ongoing demand for employees in the UK and Nigeria. Care workers are trusted with the responsibility of caring for vulnerable adults and children. Our Healthcare Assistant course is designed to provide individuals with the necessary qualifications and certification to become competent care assistants and professionals in the Global healthcare industry.

Then following modules are aligned in the Health Education England Approved Curriculum:
  1. Understand your Role
  2. Your Personal Development
  3. Duty of Care
  4. Equality and Diversity
  5. Work in a person-centered way
  6. Communication
  7. Privacy and Dignity
  8. Fluids and Nutrition
  9. Awareness of Mental Health, Dementia and Learning disabilities
  10. Safe Guarding Adults
  11. Safe Guarding Children
  12. Basic Life Support
  13. Health and Safety
  14. Handling Infromation
  15. Infection, Prevention and Control

FAQs

How long will this whole process take?
The certificate is aimed at workers joining our adult social organisation who have no previous experience. Some employers may ask current staff members to complete some of the Care Certificate standards.
How long does the certificate take to complete?
On average, it will take about 4 weeks to complete all 15 standards, internship inclusive.
How is the Care certificate assessed?
To achieve all 15 standards of the certificate, candidate(s) must acquire the knowledge and demonstrate competentce. Knowledge and understanding are assessed by asking candidate(s) to describe, explain, define, list, or identify. This can be based on written or verbal evidence, including written questions or case studies. To demonstrate competence, candidate(s) will be observed by assessor(s) in a real-life environment. This allows the assesor to see if the candidate(s) can put the knowlegde they have learnt into practice.
How do I assess the Care certificate training online?
You can access the Care Certification online when you register.
